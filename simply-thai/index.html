A Place to Meet and Eat

Looking for a great spot to enjoy Thai food with friends and family? Check out SIMPLY THAI.


We're right in the heart of Bloor West Village, offering delicious Thai dishes in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. Our friendly staff is here to make your visit special.


Our menu has all the Thai classics you love, with plenty of options for everyone - whether you're into chicken, beef, seafood, or prefer vegetarian dishes. We're proud that Toronto Sun readers voted us the city's best Thai restaurant back in 2003, and we've been living up to that standard ever since.


Come on by and give us a try. We think you'll love it here.
